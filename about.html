This site stores the lecture notes of the ACM ICPC study group in UC Davis.
All articles are under CC BY-NC 4.0 license.

The UC Davis ICPC club is an organization welcomes all programming contest enthusiasts, experienced or not.
We will meet weekly to practice problems together and review lectures given by club members to prepare for the ICPC contest held every fall.
Our objective is to challenge our minds and intelligence at a higher level and grasp a deeper understanding on CS.
